My Hometown

My hometown is the regency of Tuban. Tuban is one of 38 regencies and cities in the administrative area of East Java Province. The Tuban Regency area is located on the northern coast of Java Island. Tuban is called ‘Kota Wali because Tuban is one of the cities in Java that became the center of the spread of Islamic teachings. Tuban is also known as the City of a Thousand Caves because of its location in the Northern Limestone Mountains. The famous caves in Tuban are Goa Akbar, Goa Putri Asih, etc . Tuban’s regional boundaries: north of the Java sea, east of Lamongan, south of Bojonegoro, and west of Rembang and Blora, Central Java.
