Foods in Tuban

Tuban has many signature foods and snacks. For example is the crab curry or kari rajungan.

processed using selected spices that are cooked so that the results become a special food called Kari Rajungan. This food is famous for its delicacy and savory taste. This dish is very easy to find, in various places to eat in Tuban, they always provide this food menu.

Another popular snack in Tuban is the dumbek

Dumbek is a soft food, that is wrapped in palm leaves, and the appearance of this traditional snack is oval and long. The shape of this Dumbek snack is almost the same as the shape of a trumpet. The basic ingredients for making this Dumbek use brown sugar, rice flour, salt, pandan leaves, coconut milk and grated coconut meat wrapped around siwalan leaves. It has a sweet and savoury taste that makes it popular among children
